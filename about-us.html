What is PETALS?

Petals is an academy striving to improve digital literacy. We focus on enhancing creativity, critical thinking, logical reasoning, Problem-Solving Skills and decision-making by implementing STEM based learnings. Thus, enabling kids to be future ready.

What do we do?

We conduct classes on the following for various age groups:

  • Animation
  • Game Development
  • App Development
  • Website Design
  • Arduino
